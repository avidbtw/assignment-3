ABOUT ME

Illumitati 2023

Illumitati is the alias for artist and director Tati Bruening. She is widely known for her work photographing prominent figures in Gen Z and her unique visual storytelling. Tati is at the forefront of pop culture creating viral moments across the web including the "Make Instagram Instagram Again" petition, as well as trans activist Dylan Mulvaney's face reveal. She has created a community of millions of followers between both Instagram and Tik Tok.

Her mission is to help tell the stories of unique individuals through visual rhetoric while bringing her audience into the "Tati-verse". Her unique aesthetic of minimalistic storytelling is quickly expanding into the physical world through future photo book publications as well as fashion collaborations.
